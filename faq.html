Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our post-wedding hooley

When and where is the party?

Our post-wedding hooley will take place on Saturday, 13th September 2025 from 3PM - Late at Pot Duggans in Ennistymon, Co. Clare.

What should I wear?

This is a casual celebration! Think smart casual - comfortable clothes you can dance in. No need for formal wear - we want everyone to feel relaxed and ready for a good time.

Will there be food and drinks?

Yes! There will be little nibbles and large pints. Don't come hungry but do come thirsty! We'll have a great selection of drinks and some tasty bites to keep you going through the night.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Plus ones are welcome, but they need to be good craic! If you'd like to bring someone, just let us know when you RSVP so we can plan accordingly.

Are children welcome?

Kids are welcome, but they need to be in bed early! This is going to be a late night celebration, so please plan accordingly if you're bringing little ones.

What's the vibe of the party?

Think relaxed, fun, and full of craic! We're celebrating in a beautiful Irish pub with music, dancing, good drinks, and even better company. It's all about having a great time with our favorite people.

How do I get to Pot Duggans?

Pot Duggans is located in the heart of Ennistymon, Co. Clare. It's easily accessible by car, and there's parking available nearby. If you're coming from further away, check out our "Things to Do" page for accommodation suggestions in the area.

Do you have any dietary requirements I should know about?

Please let us know of any dietary requirements when you RSVP! We want to make sure everyone can enjoy the nibbles we'll have available.

Will there be music and dancing?

Absolutely! We'll have music and plenty of space for dancing. Come ready to show off your moves (or lack thereof - we don't judge!).

What time should I arrive?

We're starting at 3PM, but feel free to arrive anytime that works for you. It's a relaxed celebration, so come when you can and stay as long as you like!

Do I need to bring a gift?

Your presence is the only present we need! We're already married and just want to celebrate with our friends and family. Just bring yourself and your dancing shoes!
